Interests: Health economics, regulation, environmental economics, substance abuse, access to care
Alex Hollingsworth is a health economist with interests in how regulations can affect public health. Hollingsworth joined the O'Neill school in the fall of 2015 after completing his PhD in Economics at the University of Arizona.
